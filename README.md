🍔  Swiggy Business Performance Dashboard
📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Swiggy food delivery transactions using SQL and Power BI. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making.
The dashboard provides visibility into revenue performance, city-wise contribution, restaurant performance, food category trends, customer ratings, and operational KPIs.
________________________________________
🎯 Business Problem
Food delivery platforms generate large volumes of transactional data every day. Business teams require a centralized dashboard to monitor:
•	Revenue performance
•	Order volume
•	Restaurant contribution
•	Food category demand
•	Customer experience metrics
•	Operational efficiency
This dashboard was developed to address these business requirements through interactive analytics.

🔍 Key Business Insights
•	Bangalore contributed approximately 34% of total revenue.
•	September recorded the highest monthly sales.
•	Food Factory emerged as the top-performing restaurant.
•	Biryani generated the highest category revenue.
•	Average customer rating remained stable at 3.75.
